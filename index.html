About USF Poker Club

Since our founding, the USF Poker Club has been dedicated to creating a vibrant community where students can learn, practice, and master the strategic game of poker. We welcome players of all skill levels, from complete beginners to seasoned veterans.

Our club focuses on developing critical thinking, probability analysis, and decision-making skills that extend far beyond the poker table. Through regular meetings, tournaments, and educational workshops, we create an environment where members can grow both as players and as strategists.
